About the System

This Attendance Maintenance System is designed specifically for blue collar workers, focusing on seamless attendance tracking at construction or industrial sites. It simplifies real-time tracking, reduces manual errors, and brings transparency to daily operations.

Key Features

  • Worker Check-in & Check-out System
  • Role-based Dashboards (Admin, Site Lead, Worker)
  • Project-based Worker Allocation
  • Attendance Verification by Admin
  • Real-time Monitoring
  • Attendance approval by sitelead.
  • Registration to website restriction to siteLead and worker.

Visual Overview

Admin Dashboard

Manage projects, assign workers, and verify attendance records.

Worker Interface

Simple UI for workers to log their entry and exit efficiently.

Site Lead Panel

Site leads manage daily attendance and worker performance.

Our Mission

To empower labor-intensive industries with efficient digital solutions, ensuring fair attendance tracking and enhancing workforce accountability through modern web-based tools.
